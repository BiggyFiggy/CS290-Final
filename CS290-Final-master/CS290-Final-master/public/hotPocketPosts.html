A hot pocket made me cry.

The other night, I was starving, and had very little food. Thankfully, my roommate let me have one of her hot pockets to snack on. I gleefully heated up the heavenly treat and rushed back to my room to devour it. Since I was super starving, I didn't give it enough time to cool down, and the lava within poured into my mouth. I needed food in my stomach, so I powered through it with teary eyes.
